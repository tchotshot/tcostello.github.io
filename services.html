We are Art of Music

We work with musicians and bands, small and large. We help them with the artwork of their Albums, Concert Posters or any advertising.

Album Art

This is where we create album covers that can be used on SoundCloud, Itunes, Spotify and every other popular music site. If specifically requested we could even create a specific artwork for Vinyls.

 

Poster Art

Our poster designs would be for when you want to debut a show, or a tour. You can also use this to make a poster to sell at the shows and concerts you perform at.

 

Merchandise

We work with merchandise companies to design apparel, posters, souvenirs and any other items you would want to sell at your shows to help advertise your music
