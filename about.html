We are the Art of Music

We work with musicians and bands, small and large. We help them with the artwork of their Albums, Concert Posters or any advertising.

About Us

This is the first company to exclusively focus on Musician/Band Branding.  Most musicians will use freelancers which could be rather incosistent, while this company will be a consistent partner with musicians. It will make the process of building a music career so much easier as there will be a company to refer to.

About The Owner

He started this company when he was graduating with his Associates at age 21. He communicated with recording studios in the Chicago area to see if they had one company they referred artists to for when they need artwork done. The recording studios said they only hire freelancers to work on the artwork, so that is when Art of Music was created.
